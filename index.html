Service Deep Dive

Equipment Diagnostics

Pool equipment issues can develop gradually, making it difficult to spot problems early.

Signs Your Pool Equipment Needs Attention:

  • ! Noisy Pumps: Unusual sounds like grinding or squealing indicate that internal components may be damaged or worn.
  • ! Weak Water Flow: If water circulation is inconsistent, your pool’s filtration system may be underperforming.
  • ! Temperature Inconsistencies: A malfunctioning pool heater can lead to uneven water temperatures, affecting comfort.
  • ! Higher Energy Bills: Equipment that isn’t operating efficiently often consumes more energy, increasing utility costs.

Systems We Repair & Maintain:

At Justin's OC Pool & Spa, we handle a wide range of hardware, ensuring all components work together seamlessly:

  • ✓ Pumps: From repairing impellers to replacing motors, we keep your pump running efficiently.
  • ✓ Filters: We clean, repair, and replace sand, cartridge, and DE filters to maintain perfect water clarity.
  • ✓ Heaters: Expert troubleshooting for common issues like ignition failure and thermostat malfunctions.
  • ✓ Chlorinators & Salt Systems: Complete diagnostics on sanitizer cells to manage chemical consistency.
  • ✓ Automatic Cleaners: Maintenance on robotic and suction-based vacuums to keep the pool debris-free.
